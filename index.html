    Frequently Asked Questions

    Luxury Furniture Questions

    We're committed to delivering premium furniture with exceptional craftsmanship and customer service. Here are answers to the questions our clients ask most often.

    Need More Help?

    Our furniture experts are happy to assist you with product selection, custom furniture, interior design consultation, pricing, delivery, and after-sales support.

    Do you provide custom furniture?
    Yes. We design and manufacture custom furniture according to your room dimensions, preferred materials, colors, finishes, and functional requirements.
    How long does manufacturing take?
    Most custom furniture orders require approximately 7–10 working days depending on the product complexity and order quantity.
    Do you provide installation?
    Yes. Our professional installation team ensures every product is assembled and installed safely with a premium finish.
    Is warranty available?
    Yes. Selected furniture products include warranty coverage for manufacturing defects and hardware, subject to our warranty terms.
    Which cities do you serve?
    We serve customers in major cities across Nepal. Please contact us to confirm delivery and installation availability in your location.
    Do you provide interior design only?
    Absolutely. We provide complete interior design consultation even if you do not purchase furniture from us.
    What materials do you use?
    We use premium quality plywood, laminates, fabrics, rexine, and branded hardware for long-lasting durability.
