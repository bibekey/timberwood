ABOUT TIMBERWOOD

Founded in 2022, Timber Wood Pvt. Ltd. is a legally registered furniture manufacturing and interior solutions company in Nepal, dedicated to creating elegant, durable, and functional furniture for homes, offices, hotels, and commercial spaces..

Officially incorporated on 17 August 2022 under the Companies Act, 2006, and registered with the Office of the Company Registrar, Government of Nepal, our company operates with a commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. We are also registered with Nepal's Inland Revenue Department (PAN Registered), reflecting our dedication to professional business practices.

Our Story

Every great space begins with quality furniture. With this belief, Timber Wood Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2022 with a vision to provide premium furniture and interior solutions that combine exceptional craftsmanship, modern design, and lasting durability.

Officially incorporated on 17 August 2022 under the Companies Act, 2006 and registered with the Office of the Company Registrar, Government of Nepal, Timber Wood was founded to meet the growing demand for furniture that is both elegant and functional. From the beginning, our goal has been simple: to create products that improve everyday living while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service.

What started as a commitment to craftsmanship has grown into a trusted furniture brand serving homeowners, businesses, and commercial projects across Nepal. Every sofa, bed, dining set, wardrobe, office workstation, and custom-made piece reflects our passion for excellence and attention to detail.

Today, Timber Wood continues to evolve by embracing innovative designs, premium materials, and skilled workmanship. We believe that furniture is more than a product, it is part of the memories people create in their homes and workplaces. This philosophy inspires us to deliver pieces that offer comfort, functionality, and timeless style.

As we move forward, our commitment remains unchanged: to build lasting relationships with our customers through trust, quality, innovation, and outstanding service, while becoming one of Nepal's leading names in premium furniture and interior solutions.
